How much ESG data are they sharing?

We’ve analyzed the top 100’s ESG reporting and scored each company on how much information they share.

Apparel companies need to share more ESG data

On average the top 100 Apparel companies share only 10% of key environmental, social and governance indicators.

Nearly a third of the top 100 don’t say anything at all about their actions to protect the environment or achieve gender equality, for example.

Deeds not words

One big problem is companies may have a policy in place to improve their impact, but don’t follow up to make sure it’s put into action. Having good policies positively skew a company's ESG score data without necessarily achieving a real world result.
