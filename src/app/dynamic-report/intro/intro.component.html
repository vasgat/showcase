The top 100 apparel companies: a case study

Apparel is a sector that has made significant steps in terms of transparency over the past decades. It's one of the sectors that shares comparatively a lot of sustainability data.

But, given the sector's size and worldwide impact, we still know relatively little. To achieve the SDGs, for example, we need to know more; much more.
