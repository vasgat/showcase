What does transparency mean for the top 100 apparel companies?

This case study compiles information on the top 100 apparel companies' supply chain transparency and Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) data. It builds a picture of what is published to the public and what not.
A CASE STUDY

Apparel is a sector that has made significant steps in terms of transparency over the past decades. It's one of the sectors that shares comparatively a lot of sustainability data. But, given the sector's size and worldwide impact, we know relatively little. To achieve the SDGs, for example, we need to know much more.
