A vital part of ESG data disclosures is Supply Chain Transparency

Sharing the name and exact location of suppliers may not sound transformational. But it is fundamental on creating better working conditions and preventing modern slavery.

The good news is more companies are reporting

In recent years, community pressure on big apparel companies to make their supply chains more transparent has seen some success. The number of the top apparel companies reporting their suppliers more than doubled from 10 to 23 between 2017 and 2020.
