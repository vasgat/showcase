Create your own powerful data ecosystem that paints a picture

Access to reliable and well-presented companies' ESG data or supply chain information is essential for those interested in improving the world, but often it's not available or too expensive. Even if we have the data, it can be hard to identify the signal among the noise or know where to focus our attention.

We made apparel top 100 to show Wikirate.org's potential to use data to create a sector snapshot that is valuable to multiple audiences. WikiRate can co-create your open data ecosystem that makes clear important information with visualizations and analyses. With this, we can see how companies' operations help or harm the world.

We are a community-driven platform; without the community, Apparel Top 100 would not be possible.

How we transform data into your open data ecosystems

01

Scoping

We look for exciting research topics to analyze and partners to work with.

02

Mapping to WikiRate

Together with partners, we map their research methodologies to the WikiRate platform and system, laying the foundation for opening up and scaling research.

03

Data Aggregation

Our NGO and university partners work alongside volunteers and students to find and add new data.

04

Data Polishing

We review the data and metrics for quality and consistency.

05

Insights Presented

We create insights that help partners and companies assess current disclosure performance and identify where more disclosure is needed.

06

Scaling and Replication

With the data and methodology stored on the WikiRate platform, the research can be scaled and reanalyzed for richer results.
