Who made Apparel 100 and why?

Apparel 100 was made by WikiRate to showcase supply chain transparency and ESG data in an engaging and accessible way. These maps and infographics create a sector snapshot that tells a story the world deserves to know.

This showcase came together with the support of the WikiRate community.
