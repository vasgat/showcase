About the project
Apparel 100 is a contribution from the Wikirate.org to help the world make more sense of ESG data. Our aim is to spur corporations to be transparent and responsive by making data about their social and environmental impacts accessible, comparable and free for all. With the support of our community, we collected [15k] data points for [174] metrics.
